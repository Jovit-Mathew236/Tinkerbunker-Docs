# Browsing Courses

Anyone can browse TinkerBunker's public course catalog without creating an account.

---

## How to Browse

1. Visit the TinkerBunker homepage.
2. Click **Courses** in the navigation bar.
3. Browse the catalog or use filters to narrow results.

---

## Filters

| Filter | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Show courses in a specific category |
| **Search** | Find courses by keyword |

---

## Course Cards

Each course card shows:

- Course title and thumbnail.
- Category.
- Brief description.

Click a card to view the full course details page.

---

## Next Steps

To enroll in a course, you need to [create an account](../getting-started/logging-in.md) or sign in.
