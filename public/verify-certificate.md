# Verifying Certificates

TinkerBunker certificates can be verified by anyone using a unique certificate ID.

---

## How to Verify

1. Go to the TinkerBunker certificate verification page.
2. Enter the **Certificate ID** (printed on the certificate).
3. Click **Verify**.

---

## What You Will See

If the certificate is valid:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Student Name** | The person who earned the certificate |
| **Course Name** | The course that was completed |
| **Completion Date** | When the course was finished |
| **Certificate ID** | Unique identifier for this certificate |

If the certificate ID is invalid or not found, you will see an error message.
