# 🏫 Manage Schools

Invite institutes and manage your school network.

---

## ➕ Invite a School

1. Go to **Schools** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite School**.
3. Enter the school name and admin email.
4. Assign a seat package.
5. Click **Send Invite**.

---

## 📋 Manage Existing Schools

| Action | How |
| --- | --- |
| View details | Click any school from the list |
| Edit info | Open school > click **Edit** |
| Assign seats | Open school > go to **Seats** tab |
| Remove school | Open school > click **Remove** |

{% hint style="warning" %}
Removing a school revokes all its seats and student access.
{% endhint %}
