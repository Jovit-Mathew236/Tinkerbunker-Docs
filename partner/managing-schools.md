# Manage Schools

---

## Invite a School

1. Go to **Schools** in the navigation bar.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. Enter the institute name and primary email.
4. Send the invitation.

The institute admin receives an email to join TinkerBunker under your partner account.

---

## What You Can Manage

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Institute Details** | View and edit school information |
| **Grades & Divisions** | Configure academic structure |
| **Remote Devices** | Manage quiz hardware for the school |
| **Seat Allocation** | Assign licenses to the school |

{% hint style="info" %}
Institutes cannot self-register. They must be invited by a partner.
{% endhint %}
