# Create Your Account

---

## Choose Your Signup Type

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="Personal Signup" %}

Creates a standalone account not linked to any school.

1. Go to the TinkerBunker signup page.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Click **Sign Up**.
4. Verify your email using the link or code sent to your inbox.
5. You're in.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/personal-signup-form.png" alt="Personal Signup"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% endtab %}
{% tab title="Institute Signup" %}

Links your account to a school and assigns you a role.

1. Go to the TinkerBunker signup page.
2. Select **Institute Signup**.
3. Enter your name, email, and password.
4. Choose your role — **Student** or **Teacher**.
5. Search for and select your school.
6. Click **Sign Up**.
7. Verify your email.
8. Wait for your institute admin to approve your request.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/institute-signup-form.png" alt="Institute Signup"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
Institute signups require approval. You won't have full access until your admin approves you.
{% endhint %}

{% endtab %}
{% endtabs %}

## Google Signup

Click **Sign up with Google** to create your account in one step. No email verification needed.

For institute signups via Google, you'll still need to select your role and school after authentication.

---

## Invitation-Only Roles

Not all roles can self-register. Some require an invitation from a higher-level role.

| Role | How to Join |
|---|---|
| **Student / Teacher** | Self-register and pick your school. Approval required. |
| **Guardian** | Self-register, then link to your child's account. |
| **Institute** | Invitation only — from a Partner. |
| **Partner** | Invitation only — from the Sales team. |
| **Public** | No account needed. |
