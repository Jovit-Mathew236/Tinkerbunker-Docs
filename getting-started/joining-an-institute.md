# Join an Institute

---

Already have a TinkerBunker account? You can request to join a school without creating a new account.

---

## How to Join

1. Open your **Profile** settings.
2. Click **Add Institute Profile**.
3. Choose your role — **Student** or **Teacher**.
4. Search for and select the institute.
5. Submit the request.

---

## Approval Process

| Status | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Your request is waiting for the institute admin to review |
| **Approved** | You now have access to the institute's classrooms and courses |
| **Rejected** | Your request was denied. You can reapply or try a different institute. |

{% hint style="info" %}
While your request is pending, you can still use the platform with your existing roles.
{% endhint %}
