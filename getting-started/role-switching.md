# Switching Roles

If your account has multiple roles, you can switch between them without logging out.

---

## How to Switch

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Switch Role**.
3. Choose the role you want to activate.

Your dashboard and navigation will update immediately.

---

## Common Admin Combinations

| Combination | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Admin Creator + Admin Publisher | Create and publish content end-to-end |
| Sales + Admin Publisher | Manage partners and oversee publishing |
| Super Admin + any role | Full access with the ability to test other role views |

{% hint style="info" %}
Switching roles does not affect your data or permissions. Each role has its own dashboard and navigation.
{% endhint %}
