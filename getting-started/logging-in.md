# Logging In

Admin accounts use the same login flow as all other TinkerBunker users.

---

## How to Log In

1. Go to the TinkerBunker login page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Sign In**.

You will land on the dashboard for your current active role.

![Login Page](../.gitbook/assets/login-page.png)

---

## Google Sign-In

Click **Sign in with Google** to authenticate with your Google account. No password needed.

---

## After Login

Your dashboard depends on your active role. Use the profile menu to switch roles if you have more than one.

- Sales users see the partner management dashboard.
- Admin Creators see the course builder.
- Admin Publishers see the review queue.
- Super Admins see the full course and category management view.
