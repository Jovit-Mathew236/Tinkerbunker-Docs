# ❤️ Favorites

Save courses you love for quick access.

---

## How To

- Tap the **heart icon** on any course card or detail page
- Tap it again to unfavorite

---

## Where To Find Them

Go to **Courses** → switch to the **Favorites** tab.

{% hint style="info" %}
Unfavoriting a course does not unlink it from any classroom.
{% endhint %}
