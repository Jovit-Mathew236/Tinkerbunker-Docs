# Your Dashboard

The teacher dashboard is your home screen after logging in.

![Teacher Dashboard](../.gitbook/assets/teacher-dashboard.png)

---

## Navigation Bar

| Tab | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Classroom** | View and manage your classrooms |
| **Courses** | Browse the course catalog |
| **Tests** | Create and manage tests and assignments |
| **Stats** | View classroom and student performance |

---

## Quick Actions

- Click a **classroom** to see students and linked courses.
- Click **Tests** to create a new test or manage assignments.
- Click **Stats** to review student performance.
