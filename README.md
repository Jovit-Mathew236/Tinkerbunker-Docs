## How Users Join TinkerBunker

| Role | How to Join |
|---|---|
| **Student / Teacher** | Self-register and pick your school. Your institute approves you. |
| **Guardian** | Self-register, then link to your child's account. |
| **Institute** | Invited by a Partner. |
| **Partner** | Invited by the Sales team. |
| **Public** | No account needed. Browse freely. |

{% hint style="info" %}
Students and Teachers can self-register, but need their institute to approve them.
{% endhint %}
