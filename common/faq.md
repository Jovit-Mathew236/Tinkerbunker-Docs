# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

**What is TinkerBunker?**
TinkerBunker is an EdTech platform for managing courses, tests, classrooms, and educational workflows across institutions.

**How many roles does TinkerBunker have?**
There are 11 roles. The admin-facing roles are Sales, Admin Creator, Admin Publisher, and Super Admin.

---

## Sales

**How do I invite a partner?**
Go to Partners, click Invite Partner, and enter their details. They will receive an email invitation.

**Can partners self-register?**
No. Partners can only join through a Sales invitation.

---

## Admin Creator

**Can I edit a course after submitting it for review?**
No. You need to wait for the publisher to either approve it or request changes. If changes are requested, you can edit and resubmit.

**What happens to a course in Draft status?**
It stays private and invisible to everyone except you until you submit it for review.

---

## Admin Publisher

**How do I manage Sales users?**
Go to the Sales section from the sidebar. You can invite new Sales users and manage existing ones.

**Can I edit a course directly?**
No. You can only approve or request changes. The Admin Creator makes the edits.

---

## Super Admin

**Can I delete any course?**
Yes. Super Admins can delete any course regardless of status or creator.

**How do I manage categories?**
Go to Categories from the sidebar. You can create, edit, and delete categories from there.
