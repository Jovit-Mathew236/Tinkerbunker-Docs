# Troubleshooting

---

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Can't log in | Check your email and password. Use [Forgot Password](../getting-started/forgot-password.md) if needed. |
| Not receiving verification email | Check your spam folder. Request a new email from the signup screen. |
| Account pending approval | Your institute admin hasn't approved you yet. Contact them directly. |
| Course not showing up | Make sure you're enrolled. Private courses require a direct link or classroom assignment. |
| Certificate not generated | Complete all chapters and pages in the course. Partial completion doesn't count. |
| Payment failed | Check with your bank first. Refunds take 5-7 business days. |
| Remote quiz not working | Make sure the USB receiver is connected and all remotes are paired. |
| Can't switch roles | You may only have one role. Check your profile to see available roles. |
| Test timer ran out | Answers saved up to that point are submitted automatically. |
| Dashboard is empty | You may not have enrolled in any courses yet. Go to Courses to get started. |

{% hint style="info" %}
If your issue isn't listed here, contact your institute admin or reach out to support.
{% endhint %}
