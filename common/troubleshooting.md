# Troubleshooting

---

## Login Issues

**I cannot log in.**
Check that your email and password are correct. If you forgot your password, use the password reset flow on the login page.

**I logged in but see the wrong dashboard.**
You may be on a different role. Click your profile icon and switch to the correct role.

---

## Course Issues

**My course is stuck in Pending Review.**
Contact the Admin Publisher to review your submission. They may not have seen it yet.

**I submitted a course but need to make changes.**
You cannot edit a course in Pending Review status. Ask the publisher to request changes, or wait for their feedback.

---

## Partner and Sales Issues

**My partner invitation was not received.**
Check the email address you used. The invitation may also be in the partner's spam folder.

**A partner cannot see their assigned courses.**
Make sure the courses are in Published status and have been assigned to that partner.

---

## General

**Pages are loading slowly.**
Clear your browser cache and try again. If the issue persists, contact the technical team.

**I see an error message I do not understand.**
Take a screenshot of the error and contact your Super Admin or the technical support team.
