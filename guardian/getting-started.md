# Getting Started

---

As a guardian, you can monitor your child's learning progress on TinkerBunker.

---

## What You Can Do

- View your child's enrolled courses and completion status.
- Check test scores and performance trends.
- See classroom details and linked teachers.

---

## How to Get Started

1. Create an account on TinkerBunker.
2. Link your child's student account.
3. Start monitoring from your dashboard.

→ [Link your child](linking-students.md)
