# Managing Courses

Courses are assigned to your institute by the partner or platform administrator. As an institute admin, you can view course details, link courses to classrooms, and monitor adoption across your institute.

---

## Viewing Available Courses

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Courses**.
2. The course list displays all courses currently assigned to your institute.

Each course card shows:

| Field            | Description                                         |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Course Name**  | Title of the course.                                |
| **Subject**      | Subject area (e.g., Mathematics, Science).          |
| **Grade**        | Target grade level.                                 |
| **Modules**      | Number of modules / chapters in the course.         |
| **Linked To**    | Number of classrooms this course is linked to.      |
| **Status**       | Active or Inactive.                                 |

## Course Detail View

Click on any course to open its detail view. The detail view includes:

- **Overview** — course description, objectives, and metadata.
- **Module Breakdown** — list of modules with topic counts.
- **Linked Classrooms** — classrooms where this course is currently active.
- **Adoption Stats** — percentage of students who have started or completed the course.

{% hint style="info" %}
Institute admins cannot edit course content. Content is managed by the course creator or platform administrator. If you notice issues with course material, contact your partner or support.
{% endhint %}

---

## Linking a Course to a Classroom

1. From the course detail view, click **Link to Classroom**.
2. Select one or more classrooms from the dropdown.
3. Confirm the selection.

Alternatively, you can link courses from within the classroom detail view. See [Managing Classrooms](managing-classrooms.md#linking-courses).

{% hint style="warning" %}
A course can only be linked to classrooms that match its target grade level. If the grade does not match, the classroom will not appear in the selection dropdown.
{% endhint %}

---

## Unlinking a Course

1. From the course detail view, navigate to **Linked Classrooms**.
2. Click the **Unlink** button next to the classroom.
3. Confirm the action.

{% hint style="danger" %}
Unlinking a course immediately removes student access to that course in the affected classroom. Progress data is retained but hidden until the course is re-linked.
{% endhint %}

---

## Course Adoption Metrics

The **Adoption Stats** panel on each course detail page provides:

- **Total Students with Access** — students across all linked classrooms.
- **Students Started** — students who have opened at least one module.
- **Students Completed** — students who have completed all modules.
- **Average Progress** — mean completion percentage across all students.

These metrics help you understand how effectively a course is being utilized within your institute.
