# Institute Stats

---

Click **Stats** in the navigation bar to view performance data across your institute.

| Metric | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Student Performance** | Average scores and completion rates |
| **Course Adoption** | How many students are enrolled per course |
| **Classroom Activity** | Engagement levels across classrooms |

![Institute Stats](../.gitbook/assets/institute-stats-overview.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
Stats update automatically as students complete courses and tests.
{% endhint %}
