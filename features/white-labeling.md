# White Labeling

TinkerBunker supports white labeling, allowing partners to customize the platform's appearance for their schools.

---

## What Can Be Customized

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| **Logo** | Replace the TinkerBunker logo with the partner's branding |
| **Colors** | Customize primary and accent colors |
| **Domain** | Use a custom domain for the platform |
| **Login Page** | Branded login screen for the partner's users |

---

## Who Can Configure

White labeling is managed at the partner level and configured by the Super Admin or through partner settings.

---

## How It Works

1. Super Admin or Partner configures branding in the partner settings.
2. Schools under that partner see the custom branding.
3. Students and teachers logging in through that partner's domain see the branded experience.
