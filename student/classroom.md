# Classroom

The **Classroom** section on TinkerBunker connects you with your teacher and fellow students. Your teacher assigns you to a classroom, and from there you can access linked courses, assignments, and class information.

---

## Accessing Your Classroom

1. Click **Classroom** in the top navigation bar.
2. Your assigned classroom is displayed with all relevant details.

![Student Classroom View](.gitbook/assets/student-classroom.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
A student is assigned to a classroom by their teacher. You cannot join or leave a classroom on your own. If you do not see a classroom, contact your teacher or administrator.
{% endhint %}

---

## What Information Is Shown

The classroom page provides a consolidated view of everything related to your class.

| Section | Description |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Classroom Name** | The name of the classroom as set by your teacher. |
| **Teacher** | The name of the teacher managing this classroom. |
| **Students** | A list of all students in the classroom. |
| **Linked Courses** | Courses that your teacher has associated with this classroom. |
| **Assignments** | Tests and tasks assigned to the classroom by the teacher. |

---

## Linked Courses

Your teacher can link specific courses to the classroom. These courses appear under the **Linked Courses** section. Clicking a linked course takes you directly to the course content.

{% hint style="info" %}
Linked courses may include private courses that are not visible in the public catalog. Being part of a classroom gives you access to these courses automatically.
{% endhint %}

---

## Assignments

Assignments assigned to your classroom appear here as well as on your dashboard under **Upcoming**. Each assignment entry shows:

- Assignment/test title
- Assigned date
- Due date (if set)
- Status (Not Started, In Progress, Completed, Graded)

Click on an assignment to view its details or to start the test.

---

## Classmates

You can see a list of students in your classroom. This is informational and helps you know who is part of your learning group.

| Field | Description |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Name** | Student's display name. |
| **Status** | Whether they are active or not. |

{% hint style="warning" %}
Student-to-student messaging is not available within the classroom. For communication, use external channels as directed by your teacher.
{% endhint %}

---

## Classroom and Your Dashboard

Any activity from your classroom — such as new assignments, grade releases, or course additions — is reflected on your **Dashboard** under the **Upcoming** and **Notifications** sections. You do not need to check the classroom page separately for updates; the dashboard keeps you informed.
