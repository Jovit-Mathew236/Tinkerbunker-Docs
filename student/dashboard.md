# Student Dashboard

The **Student Dashboard** is your central hub on TinkerBunker. Once you log in, this is the first screen you see. It provides a quick snapshot of your learning journey, upcoming activities, and important updates.

![Student Dashboard](.gitbook/assets/student-dashboard.png)

---

## Dashboard Sections

| Section | Description |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **My Courses** | Displays all courses you are currently enrolled in, with progress indicators. |
| **Upcoming** | Shows upcoming tests, assignments, and deadlines assigned by your teacher. |
| **Notifications** | Lists recent alerts such as new assignments, grade releases, and announcements. |

---

## Navigation Bar

The top-level navigation bar is visible on every screen and gives you quick access to the main areas of TinkerBunker.

| Nav Item | Destination |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Classroom** | View your assigned classroom, classmates, and linked courses. |
| **Courses** | Browse the course catalog, view enrolled courses, and discover new ones. |
| **Stats** | Access your progress, proficiency metrics, leaderboard, and growth data. |

{% hint style="info" %}
The dashboard automatically refreshes when you log in. Any newly assigned tests or courses will appear under **Upcoming** and **Notifications** respectively.
{% endhint %}

---

## My Courses

The **My Courses** section shows cards for each course you are enrolled in. Each card displays:

- Course title and thumbnail
- A progress bar indicating how much of the course you have completed
- The last chapter or page you visited

Clicking on a course card takes you directly to the course content, resuming where you left off.

{% hint style="info" %}
Courses you have completed will display a **Completed** badge. You can still revisit them at any time.
{% endhint %}

---

## Upcoming

The **Upcoming** section aggregates time-sensitive items in chronological order:

- **Assigned Tests** — Tests your teacher has assigned, with due dates.
- **Live Quizzes** — Scheduled remote quiz sessions you need to join.
- **Course Deadlines** — Any teacher-set deadlines for course completion.

Each item shows the title, the assigning teacher, and a countdown or date.

---

## Notifications

Notifications keep you informed about activity on your account:

- New course enrollments confirmed
- Test grades released
- Certificate generated
- Classroom announcements from your teacher

{% hint style="warning" %}
Notifications are not sent via email by default. Always check your dashboard for the latest updates.
{% endhint %}

---

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard you can also perform common tasks quickly:

1. **Resume a Course** — Click the course card to jump back in.
2. **Start a Test** — Click an upcoming test to navigate to the test screen.
3. **View Stats** — Click the **Stats** nav item or the stats summary widget on the dashboard.
