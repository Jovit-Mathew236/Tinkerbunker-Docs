# 🎓 Your Dashboard

This is the first screen you see after logging in.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/student-dashboard.png" alt="Student Dashboard"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

---

## 📋 What's on the Dashboard

| Section | What You'll See |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **My Courses** | Your enrolled courses with progress bars. |
| **Upcoming** | Tests, quizzes, and deadlines from your teacher. |
| **Notifications** | New grades, certificates, and announcements. |

{% hint style="info" %}
The dashboard updates automatically every time you log in.
{% endhint %}

---

## 🧭 Navigation Bar

The nav bar appears on every screen. Use it to jump around quickly:

- 🏫 **Classroom** — Your class, classmates, and linked courses
- 📚 **Courses** — Browse, enroll, and continue learning
- 📊 **Stats** — Progress, proficiency, and leaderboard

---

## ⚡ Quick Actions

- Click a **course card** to resume where you left off.
- Click an **upcoming test** to start it.
- Click **Stats** to check your progress.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Notifications are in-app only — no emails. Check your dashboard often!
{% endhint %}
