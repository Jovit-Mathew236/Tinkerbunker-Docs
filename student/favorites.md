# Favorites

TinkerBunker lets you **favorite** courses so you can quickly find and access them later. Favoriting is a simple way to bookmark courses you are interested in or want to return to.

---

## How to Favorite a Course

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="From the Course Catalog" %}
1. Navigate to **Courses** in the navigation bar.
2. Browse or search for a course.
3. Click the **heart icon** (or **Favorite** button) on the course card.
4. The icon fills in to indicate the course is now favorited.
{% endtab %}

{% tab title="From the Course Detail Page" %}
1. Open any course by clicking on its card.
2. On the course detail page, click the **heart icon** near the course title.
3. The course is added to your favorites.
{% endtab %}
{% endtabs %}

## How to Unfavorite a Course

1. Locate the favorited course (in the catalog, detail page, or your favorites list).
2. Click the **filled heart icon** again.
3. The icon reverts to its unfilled state and the course is removed from your favorites.

{% hint style="info" %}
Unfavoriting a course does not affect your enrollment. If you are enrolled in the course, you will still have full access to it.
{% endhint %}

---

## Where to Find Favorited Courses

Your favorited courses are accessible from the **Courses** page.

| Location | How to Access |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Courses Page** | Look for a **Favorites** tab or filter on the Courses page. |
| **Course Catalog** | Favorited courses display a filled heart icon on their cards. |

---

## Use Cases for Favorites

- **Bookmarking for Later** — Found an interesting course but not ready to enroll? Favorite it.
- **Quick Access** — Favorite your most-used courses for faster navigation.
- **Wishlist** — Use favorites as a wishlist for paid courses you plan to purchase later.

{% hint style="info" %}
There is no limit to the number of courses you can favorite. Use this feature freely to organize your learning journey.
{% endhint %}
