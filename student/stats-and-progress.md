# Stats & Progress

The **Stats** section gives you a detailed view of your learning performance on TinkerBunker. It tracks your progress, measures proficiency, evaluates concept clarity, visualizes growth over time, and ranks you on a leaderboard.

---

## Accessing Stats

1. Click **Stats** in the top navigation bar.
2. Your personalized statistics dashboard loads with multiple data sections.

## Dashboard Sections

| Section | Description |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Progress Tracking** | Overall completion across all enrolled courses. |
| **Proficiency Metrics**| Skill-level assessments based on test performance. |
| **Concept Clarity** | Scores indicating how well you understand individual topics and concepts. |
| **Growth Charts** | Visual graphs showing your improvement over time. |
| **Leaderboard** | Your ranking compared to other students in your classroom or globally. |

---

## Progress Tracking

Progress tracking aggregates your course completion data.

| Metric | Description |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Courses Enrolled** | Total number of courses you are enrolled in. |
| **Courses Completed** | Number of courses fully completed. |
| **Overall Completion %** | Weighted average of progress across all active courses. |
| **Chapters Completed** | Total chapters finished across all courses. |
| **Pages Viewed** | Total individual pages viewed. |

{% hint style="info" %}
Progress is updated in real time as you navigate through course content. There is no need to manually refresh.
{% endhint %}

---

## Proficiency Metrics

Proficiency scores are calculated based on your test performance. They measure how skilled you are in the subjects you have been tested on.

| Metric | What It Measures |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Subject Proficiency** | Your skill level in each subject, derived from test scores. |
| **Average Score** | Mean score across all tests taken. |
| **Accuracy Rate** | Percentage of questions answered correctly out of total attempted. |
| **Consistency** | How stable your performance is across multiple tests. |

---

## Concept Clarity

Concept clarity scores break down your understanding at a granular level.

- Each concept or topic within a course is scored individually.
- Scores are based on questions tagged to specific concepts in tests.
- Low clarity scores highlight areas that need more attention.

| Score Range | Interpretation |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 80 - 100 | Strong understanding. |
| 60 - 79 | Good understanding, minor gaps. |
| 40 - 59 | Moderate understanding, review recommended. |
| 0 - 39 | Weak understanding, revisit the material. |

{% hint style="warning" %}
Concept clarity scores update only after you take tests that include questions mapped to those concepts. Simply completing course pages does not affect these scores.
{% endhint %}

## Growth Charts

Growth charts provide a visual timeline of your performance.

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="What Is Tracked" %}
- Test scores over time
- Proficiency improvement per subject
- Completion rate trends (weekly/monthly)
{% endtab %}

{% tab title="How to Read" %}
- The X-axis represents time (dates or test attempts).
- The Y-axis represents the metric value (score, percentage, etc.).
- An upward trend indicates improvement.
- Flat or downward trends may indicate areas requiring extra effort.
{% endtab %}
{% endtabs %}

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks students based on overall performance metrics.

| Column | Description |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Rank** | Your position relative to other students. |
| **Name** | Student display name. |
| **Score** | Aggregate performance score used for ranking. |
| **Courses** | Number of courses completed. |

{% hint style="info" %}
Leaderboard rankings are recalculated periodically. Your position may change as other students complete courses and tests.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
The leaderboard may be scoped to your classroom or the entire platform, depending on the configuration set by your teacher or administrator.
{% endhint %}

---

## Using Stats Effectively

- Review your **Concept Clarity** scores after each test to identify weak areas.
- Monitor **Growth Charts** weekly to ensure you are on an upward trajectory.
- Use the **Leaderboard** as motivation but focus on your personal growth rather than just ranking.
