# Managing Categories

Super Admins manage the course category system that organizes all content on the platform.

---

## Viewing Categories

1. Go to **Categories** from the sidebar.
2. You will see a list of all current categories.

---

## Creating a Category

1. Click **Create Category**.
2. Enter the category name and optional description.
3. Click **Save**.

New courses can now be assigned to this category.

---

## Editing a Category

1. Click on the category name.
2. Update the name or description.
3. Click **Save**.

Changes apply to all courses already in that category.

---

## Deleting a Category

1. Click the delete icon next to the category.
2. Confirm the deletion.

You cannot delete a category that still has courses assigned to it. Reassign those courses first.

{% hint style="info" %}
Categories appear in the course catalog filters. Keep them organized and meaningful.
{% endhint %}
