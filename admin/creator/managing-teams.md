# Managing Teams

Admin Creators can invite team members to help with content creation.

---

## Inviting a Team Member

1. Go to **Teams** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the member's email.
4. Select permissions.
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

---

## Team Member Permissions

| Permission | What It Allows |
|---|---|
| **Course Operation** | Create and edit courses |
| **Classroom Operation** | Manage classrooms (if applicable) |

---

## Managing Members

Click on a team member to view or update their permissions. You can also remove team members from this screen.
