# Managing Content

Admin Creators manage their course content throughout its lifecycle.

---

## Course Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Work in progress. Only visible to you. |
| **Pending Review** | Submitted for publisher review. |
| **Changes Requested** | Publisher sent it back with feedback. |
| **Approved** | Ready to be published and distributed. |

---

## Editing a Course

1. Go to **Courses** from the sidebar.
2. Click on the course you want to edit.
3. Make your changes to chapters, pages, or settings.
4. Click **Save**.

You can only edit courses in **Draft** or **Changes Requested** status.

---

## Handling Feedback

When a publisher requests changes:

1. Open the course.
2. Review the feedback notes.
3. Make the requested changes.
4. Click **Submit for Review** again.

---

## Course Analytics

Once a course is live, you can view basic engagement data:

- Number of enrollments.
- Completion rates by chapter.
- Test scores and pass rates.
