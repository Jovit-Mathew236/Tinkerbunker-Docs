# Managing Sales Teams

Admin Publishers are responsible for managing the Sales team on the platform.

---

## Inviting Sales Users

1. Go to **Sales** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Sales User**.
3. Enter the user's email address.
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

The invited user will receive an email to complete their registration as a Sales role.

---

## Sales User List

| Column | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Sales user's name |
| **Email** | Contact email |
| **Partners** | Number of partners assigned |
| **Status** | Active or pending |

---

## Managing Sales Users

Click on a sales user to:

- View their assigned partners.
- Update their profile or permissions.
- Deactivate their account if needed.

{% hint style="info" %}
Only Admin Publishers can invite and manage Sales users.
{% endhint %}
