# Reviewing Courses

Admin Publishers review courses submitted by Admin Creators. This is a key quality gate before content goes live.

---

## The Review Queue

1. Go to **Courses** from the sidebar.
2. Filter by **Pending Review** status.
3. Click on a course to open it.

---

## What to Review

| Area | What to Check |
|---|---|
| **Content** | Accuracy, completeness, and clarity of pages |
| **Structure** | Logical chapter and page ordering |
| **Tests** | Correct questions, answers, and scoring |
| **Metadata** | Title, description, category, and thumbnail |

---

## Review Actions

After reviewing a course, you have two options:

| Action | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Approve** | Course moves to Approved status and can be published |
| **Request Changes** | Course returns to the Creator with your feedback |

---

## Adding Feedback

When requesting changes:

1. Click **Request Changes**.
2. Write your feedback explaining what needs to be fixed.
3. Submit.

The Creator will see your feedback when they open the course.

{% hint style="info" %}
Be specific in your feedback. Reference the chapter or page that needs attention.
{% endhint %}
