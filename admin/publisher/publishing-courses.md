# Publishing Courses

Once a course is approved, Admin Publishers can make it live on the platform.

---

## How to Publish

1. Go to **Courses** and filter by **Approved** status.
2. Click on the course.
3. Click **Publish**.

The course is now live and available for distribution to partners and institutes.

---

## After Publishing

Published courses can be:

- Assigned to partners by Sales users.
- Enrolled in by students (if marked as public).
- Viewed in the public course catalog (if public access is enabled).

---

## Unpublishing a Course

If a course needs to be taken down:

1. Open the course.
2. Click **Unpublish** or change the status back.

Students who already enrolled will keep their progress, but no new enrollments will be accepted.
