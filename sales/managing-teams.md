# Managing Teams

Sales users can build teams by inviting team members with specific permissions.

---

## Inviting a Team Member

1. Go to **Teams** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the member's email address.
4. Select the permissions you want to grant.
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

---

## Team Member Permissions

Each team member can be given a combination of these access flags:

| Permission | What It Allows |
|---|---|
| **Partner Operation** | Manage partners |
| **Coupon Operation** | Create and manage coupons |
| **Price Operation** | Set and modify pricing |
| **Seat Operation** | Manage seat allocations |

---

## Editing Permissions

Click on a team member to view or update their permissions. Changes take effect immediately.
