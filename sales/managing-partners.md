# Managing Partners

Sales users are responsible for onboarding and managing Partners on the platform.

---

## Inviting a Partner

1. Go to **Partners** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Partner**.
3. Enter the partner's name, email, and organization details.
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

The partner will receive an email with a link to complete their registration.

---

## Partner List

Your partner list shows all partners assigned to you.

| Column | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Partner organization name |
| **Email** | Primary contact email |
| **Schools** | Number of schools managed by the partner |
| **Status** | Active or pending |

---

## Managing a Partner

Click on a partner to view their details. From there you can:

- View their schools and sub-partners.
- Check seat allocation and usage.
- Update partner information.

{% hint style="info" %}
Partners can only be invited by Sales users. They cannot self-register.
{% endhint %}
