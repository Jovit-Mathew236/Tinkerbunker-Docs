# Coupons and Pricing

Sales users can create discount coupons and manage pricing for course seats.

---

## Creating a Coupon

1. Go to **Coupons** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Coupon**.
3. Fill in the coupon details.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Code** | The coupon code users will enter |
| **Discount Type** | Percentage or fixed amount |
| **Discount Value** | The discount amount |
| **Usage Limit** | Maximum number of times this coupon can be used |
| **Expiry Date** | When the coupon expires |

4. Click **Save**.

---

## Managing Coupons

Your coupon list shows all active and expired coupons. Click on a coupon to edit it or view usage stats.

---

## Pricing

Seat pricing determines how much institutes and partners pay per course seat. Pricing can be set at the course level by Admin Publishers or at the platform level by Super Admins.

Sales users can view current pricing and coordinate with Admin Publishers to adjust it.
