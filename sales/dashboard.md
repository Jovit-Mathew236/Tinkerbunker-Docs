# Sales Dashboard

The Sales dashboard is your home screen for managing partners, teams, and course distribution.

---

## Navigation

| Tab | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Courses** | Browse all approved courses available for partner assignment |
| **Partners** | View and manage your assigned partners |
| **Teams** | Manage your sales team members |

---

## What You Can Do

- Invite and manage Partners.
- Assign courses to Partners.
- Create and manage coupons and pricing.
- Build and manage your sales team with scoped permissions.
